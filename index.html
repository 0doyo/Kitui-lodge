TOUR PACKAGES

Discover the beauty, history, and adventure that Kitui and its surroundings have to offer with our personalized tour packages. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, history buff, or adventure seeker, we offer guided tours to some of the most exciting destinations in the region.

Zambani Rock

This iconic rock formation is a must-visit in Kitui. Known for its scenic views and cultural significance, Zambani Rock offers visitors a chance to hike to the top and enjoy panoramic vistas of the surrounding landscape. According to local legend, it is said that anyone who circles the rock seven times may receive their heart’s desire—making it both an adventure and a spiritual experience.

Tsavo East National Park

Located a short drive from Kitui, Tsavo East is one of the largest national parks in Kenya, renowned for its vast savannah, wildlife, and scenic landscapes. Spot elephants, lions, giraffes, zebras, and other wildlife on a guided safari. Enjoy game drives through the iconic red-dust plains or a relaxing picnic at one of the designated areas within the park.

Kitui Man-Made Beach – Kalundu Eco Park

Kitui Man-Made Beach offers a unique recreational experience. This artificial beach, surrounded by lush greenery, features sandy shores, a tranquil water body, and scenic picnic areas. It’s a perfect spot for family outings, relaxation, or photo sessions, with activities such as paddle boating and nature walks available. The park promotes eco-tourism, making it an ideal destination for both leisure and environmental awareness.

Ikoo Valley and Nzambani Falls

Enjoy a day trip to the beautiful Ikoo Valley, where you can hike through scenic landscapes and explore Nzambani Falls. These waterfalls provide an excellent backdrop for photos, and the area is perfect for a quiet picnic and nature walks.
